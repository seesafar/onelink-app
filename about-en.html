What is OneLink?

A quick overview of the idea behind the project.

OneLink is a non-official personal project that collects the most important Saudi government e-services on a single page. Instead of searching every time or jumping between many apps and websites, the user can start from one place and quickly reach the right service.

The page simply works as a unified directory of official websites (Absher, Najiz, Qiwa, health services, traffic, business, housing, and more) â€“ with a focus on clarity, speed and simplicity.

How do you use the OneLink page?

A simple flow to get the most out of the directory.

  1. Open the main page OneLink (index-en.html or its main URL).
  2. Use the search bar to type the service name in English or Arabic (for example: Absher, Najiz, Qiwa, MOH).
  3. Or scroll to the relevant section such as health, work & labor, transport, finance, housing, tourism, etc.
  4. Click the green Open button on the card â€“ you will be taken directly to the official government website in a new tab.

Notes & disclaimers

Important clarifications about the nature of the project.

  • OneLink is not an official government service. It is a personal initiative to organize links to public e-services only.
  • All logos and names belong to their respective official entities. OneLink only points to their public websites and does not represent them.
  • No accounts, passwords or form data are stored inside OneLink. All sensitive actions are done on the official government platforms directly.
